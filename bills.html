How To Pay Bills

Store your bills in a specific place that hold only bills so you won't lose them.

Pay Them by Order of Importance

If you are low on money and don't think you will make it through the month, pay the important bills first. Things like rent, water, and electricity are needed to keep your house running so you might want to pay those first. Also, if there are bills that are due at the beginning of the month, pay those first. Then pay those due in the middle of the month, and then those due at the end of the month.

Different types of Payment

You can pay your bills through the internet or at an office that accepts the bills that you are trying to pay. Phone companies like T-Mobile, AT&T, Sprint, and Verizon let you make payments online.
